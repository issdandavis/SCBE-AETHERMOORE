## PART 1: TIMELINE

### Foundation Era (Everweave Campaign)

Events drawn primarily from the 16,412-line Everweave campaign export and the Izack Master Lorebook.

**Year 0 -- The Awakening**
- Izack wakes with no memory on impossible shores near the wreck of the Threshold Seeker (called "Polaris" in some references). He finds blue and gold fabric, a corroded brass fitting, a name obscured by salt. [Everweave L1-10]
- Emptiness is his first wound. Curiosity is his first survival response. [Obsidian 02]
- He tries to summon a golem companion from sand and seawater; his magic is limited. He creates an Unseen Servant instead. [Everweave L10-30]
- He explores the shipwreck, finds a metallic object with a maritime guild mark and a Ravencrest sigil half-buried in sand. [Everweave L94-107]
- He follows footprints inland into a dense forest. Crafts a walking stick from an oak branch, reinforces it with magic -- the earliest seed of the Chronological Nexus Staff. [Everweave L109-120]
- He discovers ley lines crisscrossing beneath the forest floor, forgotten ritual sites, and a 17-minute pulse in the forest's magical rhythm. [Everweave L195-210, L950]

**Year 0 -- Meeting Eldrin**
- Izack follows smoke to a campsite. Meets Eldrin, a weathered human cartographic thaumaturge mapping magical resonances. They exchange knowledge -- Izack's dimensional storage expertise for Eldrin's understanding of the region. [Everweave L256-300]
- Their alliance begins through mapping, study, and practical problem-solving. [Obsidian 02]

**Year 0 -- Polly's Bond**
- Izack encounters a raven with special magical power. He offers food from his dimensional storage and speaks gently. Names it Polly. [Everweave L410-520]
- The relationship begins as conversation, not obedience. [Obsidian 02]
- Polly enters early as interpreter, witness, and sharp-tongued guide.

**Year 0-1 -- The Cave of Echoes / Living Rune Sequence**
- Izack discovers the Cave of Dimensional Resonance -- basalt walls pulsing with runes, cold air and ozone, scripts older than current institutions. [Obsidian 03, Lorebook]
- First major breakthrough: Magic is language. Boundaries are active. Meaning alters outcome. [Obsidian 02]
- He develops the first interactive Floor Guardian. Creates the Chronological Nexus Staff. [Lorebook ~Age 110-122]

**Year 1 -- Clay's Creation**
- Clay emerges as the first unconscious pact. Izack is alone and asks reality for company without knowing that is what he is doing. Clay answers. [Obsidian 02-03, Novel ch09]
- Need creates relation before theory catches up. This is the deepest canon lock in the whole setting. [Obsidian 02]
- Clay begins as the simplest act of need and nameship; later Clayborn becomes harmony, tending, and shared life. [Obsidian 03]

**Year 1-2 -- Avalon's Seed**
- Izack begins shaping dimensional refuge into the seed of Avalon. Responds to uncertainty by building habitable structure. [Obsidian 02]
- Avalon starts as a floating island in subspace, grows with each expansion of Izack's understanding. First tree grows without planting. First tower rises from the ground in spiraled stone. [Everweave ~L5040-5070]
- The Leyline Gazebo is built early -- Izack gets bored of walking and builds sanctuary. It becomes shrine, research station, rest point. Under pressure, he makes shelter. [Obsidian 02]

**Year 2-3 -- The Silvermark Meeting / Aria**
- Izack meets Aria (initially called Elara/Lady Aria in the Everweave campaign, later retconned to Aria Ravencrest). At Silvermist/Silvermark, a market/keep. [Everweave ~L5140-5260]
- Their meeting: he brings a branch from the World Tree, wrapped in parchment. She touches the bark and closes her eyes. He speaks of soil and spirit, of seeds that remember and spells that listen. [Everweave]
- The necklace gift -- both flirtation and magical argument. Boundary magic as precision, not coldness. [Obsidian 03]
- Izack falls in love not with her beauty but with her burden. Their romance becomes shared work in dimensional architecture. [Everweave]

**Year 3-5 -- Academy Building**
- Founds Avalon Academy (~age 120 in Lorebook timeline). Teaches living magical systems. [Lorebook]
- Creates the Transdimensional Reality Robes (living memory interface) and the Time-Stone Ring (marriage bond). [Lorebook]
- Marries Aria. Gifted the enchanted sword to Prince Arthur; Avalon recognized as political-magical power by King Rupert. [Lorebook]

**Year 5+ -- The Family**
- Children born in order: Alexander, twins Lyra and Mira, Senna, Kael. [Obsidian 05, Novel ch09]
- Zara Millwright joins as apprentice/dragonkin -- empathic resonance, Third Thread magic. Becomes family-adjacent. [Lorebook, Character Codex]
- Grey recruited as golem commander/warrior. Marta joins as witch ally. [Cast Bible]
- Fizzle Brightcog: gnomish enchanter, friend of Izack. Saved the west conduits twice. The middle name "Fizzle" for Senna is in her honor. [Obsidian 06, Everweave L1180]
- Malzeth'irun discovered and sheltered -- demon heir, begins re-evaluation of demonfolk origins (~age 128). [Lorebook]

**Year 5+ -- The Six Sacred Tongues**
- Izack writes poems of legacy for each child in each of the sacred languages. The six tongues crystallize: KO (Kor'aelin/Intent), AV (Metadata), RU (Binding), CA (Compute), UM (Security), DR (Structure). [Everweave ~L15520-15540]
- The tongues are weighted by the golden ratio: KO=1.00, AV=1.62, RU=2.62, CA=4.24, UM=6.85, DR=11.09. [Obsidian 16]

**Year 5+ -- The World Tree Blooms**
- Ravencrest had tried for generations to make the World Tree bloom. It answered when Izack and Aria worked together -- collaboration, not command. [Obsidian 00, 14]

**Late Foundation Era -- Ame's Pact**
- Ame, the Goddess of Wisdom, manifests in response to Izack's accumulated quest for wisdom. [Obsidian 04, Everweave ~pages 300-400]
- Pact formed during subspace ecosystem creation. She blesses the Chronological Nexus Staff for temporal navigation. Creates the Crystal Garden of Ame. Plants divine seeds. [Obsidian 04]
- Ame manifests as luminous raven or shimmering veil. Her pact is mutual -- she gains a mortal anchor, implying gods need collaboration. [Obsidian 04, 10]

**Late Foundation Era -- Crystal Helix Chamber**
- Crystal helix chamber constructed over 47 realities. Temporal Resonance Array. [Obsidian 00, Cast Bible]

**The End State of the Everweave Campaign**
- Not imperial victory. It is tea, cake, children, family, and large shared outings. The final truth is domestic and relational. [Obsidian 02]

### The Decline and Kael's Fall (~500 years before the novel)

**The Void Incursions**
- Demon realm (cosmic parasite that feeds on magical/spiritual realms) intensifies attacks. Wave after wave for years. [Everweave worldbuilding, Obsidian 11]
- Kael fights in every defense. Brilliant at predicting attack vectors, routing defenses, patching vulnerabilities. But he sees the cost: consensus delay, council arguments while people die, Alexander's coordination too slow, Senna's discipline feeling like bureaucracy. [Obsidian 11]
- He gets tired. Not physically. Architecturally tired. [Obsidian 11]

**Kael's Proposal and Rejection**
- Proposes consolidating six tongues into unified authority. One mind. No committee. No delay. He is right about speed and efficiency. Wrong about error correction. [Obsidian 11]
- Council says no. Family says no. Izack says no.
- Kael hears: you would rather let the system almost fail than let me fix it.
- He was twenty-three. Had been fighting demons since fifteen. [Obsidian 11]

**The Dominion Handlers ("The Bad Kids")**
- Over two years, "friends" arrive. Young, bright, passionate about the same things. They validate Kael's grievances, amplify them, aim them at the system's load-bearing walls. [Obsidian 11, 13]
- His language changes: from "fixing the system" to "replacing the architecture." Fixing is what engineers do. Replacing is what conquerors do. [Obsidian 11]
- They disappear when Kael acts. All of them. Pattern, not proof, of Dominion involvement. [Obsidian 11]
- Goal: centralization dissolves the distributed keys that keep the Dominion locked out. [Obsidian 13, 16]

**The Erasure**
- Kael's modifications go live. Consequences land on him alone.
- The Council erases him -- devastating because it was the only option, and everyone who signed knew they were cutting away part of their family. [Obsidian 11]
- What remains: the Void Seed. Kael's thesis made persistent. "One source of truth, one mind, no disagreement, no delay, just correctness." [Obsidian 11]

**Izack Binds Himself**
- After Kael's fracture, Izack binds himself into the architecture to hold coherence. Present but not embodied. Exists in full 21D canonical state. [Obsidian 10, 13]
- He has been holding the space for the seventh silence manually for decades. That is why he is tired. [Obsidian 13, 16]

**Polly's 500-Year Vigil**
- Evolution: Years 0-100 Advisor, 100-200 Oracle, 200-300 Hermit, 300-400 Sleeping Guardian, 400-500 Awakening. [ChatGPT 4.5]
- She has buried 200 Earth refugees. Her grief "fossilized into a shape that could bear weight." [Novel ch09, Obsidian 08]

One of the most intimate lore pieces across all sources. Izack and Aria sit in the nursery (originally a calibration room), with baby Alexander asleep in the cradle. ## PART 4: KAEL -- COMPLETE THREAD

### Kael in the Everweave Campaign

In the original Everweave logs, "Kael" appears as one of Izack's early students -- "a rune brawler with combat glyph fists" -- a minor character. [Everweave ~L5063]

### Kael in the ChatGPT 4.5 Framework

A completely different character: **Kael Nightwhisper**, age 25, frontier-born, no royal blood. The NEW protagonist meant to inherit/transform the Thorne legacy after 500 years of decline. Carries dormant Third Thread potential. This is the "hero version" -- an outsider who must choose between restoration, revolution, and transformation. His companions include Lyra Stormweave, Thomas Voidscribe, Mira Dreamtender, Senna Ironroot. [ChatGPT 4.5]

### Kael in the Novel (Current Canon)

**Kael Nightwhisper Thorne** -- youngest child of Izack and Aria. The fusion of the "rune brawler student" concept with the "Nightwhisper" name from the hero framework, but completely reimagined as a tragic figure within the family.

**The character transform happened when the ChatGPT 4.5 framework was abandoned in favor of the Marcus Chen isekai.** Instead of a new-blood hero named Kael Nightwhisper, the name was given to Izack's youngest son, and the character became the story's tragic antagonist -- a brilliant child whose correct diagnosis led to catastrophic treatment.

### Key Kael Moments Across Sources

**The Garden (Obsidian 11):** Age 14, sitting in Aria's garden under the World Tree. Same garden where Alexander built perimeter circles, Lyra sabotaged them, Mira read books, Senna held route markers. Sun shining. Mother nearby. Polly watching from the gate with the expression of someone who knows how this ends. Kael is reading a book about defensive architecture and is already solving problems the Council won't notice for a decade. Happy, loved, and already running calculations.

**The Turn (Obsidian 11):** Years of fighting, years of watching consensus delay cost lives. His proposal: consolidate six tongues into unified authority. Rejected. He hears: you would rather let the system fail than let me fix it.

**The Handlers (Obsidian 11, 13):** Two years of "friends" who sharpened his grievances. Language shifted from "fix" to "replace." They disappeared when consequences landed. Pattern of Dominion involvement.

- Architecture feels grown, not built
