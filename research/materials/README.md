# Materials Research

This folder consolidates SCBE materials research into one source area.

Use it for:

- Materials science notes that may feed the AI Materials Bench.
- Fiber, optics, waveguide, and field-geometry references.
- Energy storage, thermal cycling, vacuum, and space-materials research.
- Product-safe summaries that can become front-end demos, receipts, or backend calculators.

Current entry points:

- `source-inventory.md` - where the materials research was found across Obsidian, GitHub, and the repo.
- `product-map.md` - how the research maps into sellable systems and UI surfaces.

Working rule:

Keep hazardous, unverifiable, or procedure-like chemistry out of product copy. Product pages should show outputs, calculations, diagrams, receipts, assumptions, and limits.
