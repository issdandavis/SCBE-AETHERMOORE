# Prototype 2 — Triboelectric Droplet Channel

## Purpose

Validate liquid-solid contact electrification and droplet stability before coupling to the EM branch.

## Required measurements

- input force
- stroke/displacement
- frequency
- open-circuit voltage
- loaded voltage/current
- output power vs load
- temperature
- cycle count
- failure observations

## Deliverables

- schematic
- material list
- test procedure
- raw data CSV
- plots
- pass/fail decision
- next revision notes

## Rule

Do not claim success from voltage alone. The prototype must produce usable stored energy under a defined load.
