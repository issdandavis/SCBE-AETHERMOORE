# Prototype 3 — Combined EM + Triboelectric Cell

## Purpose

This is the make-or-break integration test: the combined cell must beat the best individual channel under the same mechanical input.

## Required measurements

- input force
- stroke/displacement
- frequency
- open-circuit voltage
- loaded voltage/current
- output power vs load
- temperature
- cycle count
- failure observations

## Deliverables

- schematic
- material list
- test procedure
- raw data CSV
- plots
- pass/fail decision
- next revision notes

## Rule

Do not claim success from voltage alone. The prototype must produce usable stored energy under a defined load.
