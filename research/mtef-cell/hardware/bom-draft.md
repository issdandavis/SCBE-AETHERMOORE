# Draft Bill of Materials — M-TEF Cell

This is a bench-prototype BOM, not a flight or product BOM.

## Mechanical frame

- clear acrylic/polycarbonate test channel or printed fixture
- syringe/piston tube or linear slide
- springs or elastic return mechanism
- adjustable end stops
- vibration/exercise input fixture
- fasteners, seals, gaskets, and fluid-compatible tubing

## Electromagnetic channel

- neodymium magnets, small cylindrical or ring format
- magnet carrier/piston
- magnet wire, multiple gauges for coil trials
- coil bobbin or printed coil form
- bridge rectifiers or low-loss Schottky diodes
- storage capacitors and supercapacitor candidate
- resistor load bank

## Triboelectric channel

- PTFE / fluoropolymer tubing or channel surface
- alternate dielectric test surfaces
- conductive droplets: Galinstan/eGaIn candidate, ionic liquid, or conductive carbon suspension
- nonconductive carrier fluid candidate
- electrodes and charge-collection pads
- high-impedance measurement leads

## Magnetic-control layer

- ferrofluid or magnetorheological fluid candidate
- containment/seal material compatible with the chosen fluid
- external steering/control magnets
- shielding/spacing material to isolate the EM and control paths during tests

## Measurement equipment

- multimeter
- oscilloscope if available
- current shunt or precision resistor set
- high-impedance electrometer or charge amplifier if available
- data logger / microcontroller ADC
- scale or force gauge
- ruler/calipers for stroke length
- tachometer/frequency counter or video-based cycle counting

## Safety notes

- Liquid metals can wet or damage materials; handle with containment.
- Ferrofluids stain and can migrate through poor seals.
- Strong magnets can pinch, break, or damage electronics.
- Do not use toxic or volatile fluids in a home prototype.
- Treat high-voltage TENG outputs as measurement signals until proven safe and controlled.
