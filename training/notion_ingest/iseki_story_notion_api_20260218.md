## Core Story Premise

The Six Tongues Protocol is an isekai where magic is protocol architecture.
Marcus Chen, a systems engineer, wakes in Aethermoor and learns that magic
works through six domain-separated tongues:

- KO (Kor'aelin): control/orchestration
- AV (Avali): transport/messaging
- RU (Runethic): policy/constraints
- CA (Cassisivadan): compute/encryption
- UM (Umbroth): security/secrets
- DR (Draumric): schema/authentication
