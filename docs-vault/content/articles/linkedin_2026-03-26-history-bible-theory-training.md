# New bounded training packet: history, covenant, witness, and sanctuary language for SCBE models

This week I took a narrow slice of my local theory corpus and converted it into a governed training packet for SCBE-AETHERMOORE.

The packet is not a general lore dump. It focuses on recurring structures that matter for how the system is explained:

- covenant
- witness
- genesis control
- memory
- sanctuary-building
- historical timeline discipline

Source notes included:

- Sacred Eggs Systems Model
- Sacred Tongues - Emotional Architecture
- Everweave Timeline

The goal is not to make a model sound more mystical.

The goal is to help a model separate:

- implemented behavior
- design intention
- exploratory math
- symbolic framing

That matters because theory-heavy corpora are easy to overtrain badly. If you just dump everything in, the model starts blurring metaphor and mechanism. A bounded packet lets you keep the older material while staying honest about what is and is not production-ready.

I also published a public research page for the packet so the claim boundary stays visible:

- `research/history-bible-theory-training.html`

This is the pattern I want more of:

turn deep notes into governed packets, then train with discipline.
