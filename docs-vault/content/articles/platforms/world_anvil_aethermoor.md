# Aethermoor — World Overview

## Description

Aethermoor is a dimension governed by six sacred languages -- the Sacred Tongues -- each controlling a fundamental domain of reality. The tongues are not metaphorical; they are the physics that hold the world together.

## The Six Sacred Tongues

| Tongue | Color | Domain | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| KO | Red-gold | Intent, authority, structure | 1.00 |
| AV | Blue-silver | Transport, routing, connections | 1.62 |
| RU | Deep purple | Policy, permission, judgment | 2.62 |
| CA | White-gold | Computation, mathematics | 4.24 |
| UM | Luminous shadow-black | Security, privacy, protection | 6.85 |
| DR | Earth-brown/amber | Schema, authentication, identity | 11.09 |

The tongues are weighted by the golden ratio (phi = 1.618). Governance tongues carry more weight than structural ones. When all six braid together, they form the Harmonic Wall -- a living interference pattern hundreds of feet high that protects Aethermoor's boundary.

## Key Locations

### The Crystal Archive
Shelves grown rather than built, rising thirty feet or more. Every book glows with its own warm light and hums at a different frequency. Smells of old leather, ozone, and something unnamed.

### Crystal Corridor
Non-Euclidean crystal hallways. The same room exists in six places at once. Doors appear when approached and vanish when passed, governed by AV tongue protocols.

### The Tongue Chamber
Six pedestals of different mineral beneath a spiraling crystal dome, each holding a Sacred Tongue sphere. A seventh pedestal -- darker, older, different script -- stands separate. A 0.3-second heartbeat pulses through the room.

### The Harmonic Wall
Hundreds of feet high. A standing wave of six tongue frequencies braided into living light. Responds to names. Carries signatures. Flinches at Kael Thorne's attack patterns.

### The Training Hall
A circle pretending to be a square. Corners soften after three steps. The far wall appears 40 feet away, then 60, then 12. Errors in practice manifest as visible spatial distortion.

## Key Characters

### Marcus Chen -- The Newcomer
32-year-old software engineer from Earth. Approaches magic like debugging a production system. Trusts frameworks faster than people. Hands always half-curled like he's still typing.

### Polly -- The Archive Keeper
Twice-normal raven with obsidian eyes, graduation cap, monocle, and silk bowtie. Shifts between raven and human form. In human form: appears 20, eyes carry centuries, hair is feathers, wings fold against back. Transforms "casually, like a window being resized."

### Izack Thorne -- The Architect
Still alive, bound into the system substrate. Holds 21 dimensions together through commitment so deep it became habitat. Chalk in his hair, pipe of Fizzlecress, untouched tea.

### Kael Thorne -- The Erased
Found the exploit at 14, weaponized it by 16. Teenager face with grandfather patience. Erased 300 years ago but his pattern survived -- 17 reactivated copies purged by 72-drone swarm.

## The Seventh Tongue

In the Tongue Chamber, the seventh pedestal bears a different script. Nobody discusses it. The Archive Keeper changes the subject when asked.

## Origin

The six tongues emerged from 12,596 paragraphs of AI game logs (Everweave). The linguistic patterns were extracted and formalized into both the novel's magic system and a real AI governance framework (SCBE-AETHERMOORE).
