## Arc Locks — Environments

### 0. Marcus's Desk - LOCKED WORKING CONSTRAINT (from book cover `SixTonguesProtocol_Cover_KDP.jpg`)
- **THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT ARC LOCK. Every office/desk panel MUST match this.**
- **Palette**: dark warm tones, green monitor glow + warm amber desk lamp
- **Desk**: dark wood, NOT corporate cubicle — personal home office
- **Monitor**: ONE large curved monitor, prominent, showing green-on-black terminal text ("Welcome to Aethermoor" / system status)
- **View angle**: over-the-shoulder (Marcus's back to viewer, head visible, hunched forward)
- **Lighting**: warm desk lamp on left + green monitor glow = mixed warm/cool
- **Background**: city skyline visible through window behind monitors
- **Props on desk**: papers, notebooks, pen, coffee mug with brown residue ring
- **Environment**: hacker's home office — personal, lived-in, NOT sterile corporate
- **Marcus wears**: dark hoodie over wrinkled white button-down
- **Use for**: ALL Earth/office scenes — P03 (the desk), P07 (face in light), P38 (empty desk post-credits)
- **NEVER**: three flat monitors, corporate cubicle, sterile fluorescents, server racks

### 1. Protocol Chamber (`01_protocol_chamber.jpg`)
- **Palette**: warm gold, amber, fire orange, deep crimson
- **Elements**: 6 Sacred Tongue spheres on stone pedestals (KO=gold/fire, AV=blue/electric, RU=purple/galaxy, CA=white/light, UM=dark/void, DR=black/obsidian)
- **Floor**: golden concentric geometric sigils, sacred geometry circles
- **Walls**: tall stone pillars with glowing runic script (Sacred Tongue glyphs)
- **Lighting**: central sphere radiates golden web of energy threads connecting all 6
- **Ceiling**: vaulted dark stone, spiral opening above
- **Mood**: sacred, powerful, initiatory
- **Use for**: Protocol activation, tongue selection, governance decisions, chapter climaxes

### 2. Aethermoor Overworld (`02_aethermoor_overworld.jpg`)
- **Palette**: purple, teal, gold, green, sunset pink
- **Elements**: floating crystal islands, rope/vine bridges, luminous dragonflies, small beast creatures
- **Terrain**: rocky spires with grass/trees on top, crystalline formations (teal/cyan glow)
- **Sky**: purple aurora bands with golden energy threads, distant sunset horizon
- **Architecture**: small stone/crystal towers on islands
- **Mood**: vast, magical, alive, Studio Ghibli meets JRPG
- **Use for**: establishing shots, travel sequences, overworld transitions, chapter openers

### 3. The Archive (`03_polly_archive.jpg`)
- **Palette**: deep purple, dark blue, neon accents (cyan, magenta, green on book spines)
- **Elements**: impossibly tall bookshelves, crystalline formations at base, Polly as giant raven
- **Scale**: dwarfs humans — shelves extend beyond frame
- **Lighting**: dim ambient with neon book-spine glow and Polly's glowing eye
- **Mood**: dark, reverent, cyberpunk-sacred, overwhelming knowledge

### 4. The Gathering Tree (`04_gathering_tree.jpg`)

- **Elements**: massive central tree with Sacred Tongue script glowing on bark, courtyard feast tables, crowd, lanterns
- **Architecture**: stone arched colonnade surrounding courtyard, medieval-warm
- **Fauna**: Polly as raven perched on branch (smaller scale than Archive form)
- **Sky**: aurora borealis, floating islands in distance

### 5. The Pipeline Cavern (`05_pipeline_cavern.jpg`)

### 6. The Crystal Throne (`06_marcus_throne_smoke.jpg`)

## Panel Flex — Allowed Style Shifts

| Style | When to Use | Visual Markers |
|---|---|---|
| **Chibi/SD** | Comedy beats, embarrassment, surprise | Deformed proportions, big head, dot eyes |
| **Painterly splash** | Dramatic reveals, awe moments, emotional peaks | Loose brushstrokes, saturated color, minimal linework |
| **Sketch/memory** | Flashbacks, fragmented recall, instability | Rough lines, desaturated, vignette edges |
| **Infographic** | Lore dumps, system explanations, Protocol status | Silhouettes, diagrams, text overlays, UI elements |
| **Speed-line action** | Combat, Protocol pulses, breach events | Motion blur, radial lines, impact frames |
