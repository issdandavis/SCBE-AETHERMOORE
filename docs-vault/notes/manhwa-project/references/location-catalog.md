## Earth Locations

### Marcus's Desk (Arc Lock #0 — from book cover)
- **Palette**: Green monitor glow + warm amber desk lamp
- **Desk**: Dark wood, personal home office
- **Monitor**: ONE large curved monitor, green-on-black terminal
- **Background**: City skyline through window
- **Props**: Coffee mug (brown residue ring), papers, notebooks, pen, 3 framed photos
- **Atmosphere**: Stale coffee smell, server hum, security guard shoes below
- **Mood**: Intimate hacker cave, NOT corporate

## Aethermoor Core

### The Crystal Archive
- **Palette**: Warm amber, crystal refraction, directionless glow
- **Materials**: Crystal shelves grown not built, smooth stone floor from centuries of footsteps
- **Books**: Leather-bound, each with individual warm glow, humming at different frequencies
- **Scale**: 30ft+ ceiling or infinite, shelves stretch beyond sight
- **Atmosphere**: Old leather + ozone + unnamed third scent, polyphonic humming chord
- **Mood**: Reverent, awe-inspiring, alive

### Crystal Corridor (Non-Euclidean)
- **Palette**: Warm amber + cool blue crystal refractions
- **Materials**: Crystal walls that hum, transparent weight-bearing floors
- **Space**: Same room in six places at once. Doorways appear when approached, vanish when passed.
- **Atmosphere**: Static electricity taste, synesthetic bleeding (hum = color + temperature)
- **Mood**: Disorienting wonder, information-dense

### The Tongue Chamber / Cathedral
- **Palette**: Six tongue colors cascading from spiral dome
- **Floor**: Hexagonal dark stone + pale crystal in fractal pattern (repeats at every scale)
- **Ceiling**: Spiraling crystal dome facets
- **Center**: Six pedestals of different mineral, each holding a Sacred Tongue sphere
- **Seventh**: Empty darker older pedestal with different script (separate)
- **Atmosphere**: Six-voice polyphonic counterpoint, 0.3s heartbeat in sternum, ozone + dry stone
- **Mood**: Sacred, powerful, initiatory

### The Refectory
- **Palette**: Warm amber, intimate scale
- **Materials**: Crystal-stone geometry shaped for bodies, long tables
- **Lighting**: Hanging lamps that hum at felt frequency
- **Population**: Earth refugees + Aether-born staff (silver tattoos, patched jackets, DR wristbands)
- **Special**: Essence Brew — calibrates to need-state
- **Mood**: Survival-earned respite, communal

### The Harmonic Wall / Firewall
- **Palette**: Six-color standing wave bands (living interference pattern)
- **Scale**: Hundreds of feet high, extends beyond visible boundaries
- **Materials**: Translucent mineral + braided light, black lattice platform over void
- **Atmosphere**: Six overlaid frequencies, cold near seams (lungs clench), tremor in bones
- **Consciousness**: Responds to names, carries signatures, flinches at Kael's patches
- **Mood**: Power, protection, living defense

### The Training Hall (Hyperbolic Chamber)
- **Shape**: Circle pretending to be a square. Corners soften after 3 steps.
- **Center**: Single obsidian plinth with six language sigil glows
- **Space**: Non-Euclidean — far wall appears 40ft then 60ft then 12ft
- **Atmosphere**: Intent-responsive, errors visible as spatial distortion
- **Mood**: Pedagogical disorientation

### Infrastructure Vault / Leyline Nexus
- **Palette**: Industrial blacks, amber/red pulsing nodes, violet RU flashes
- **Materials**: Black metal nodes, cable bundles thick as tree trunks, scaffolding, grated platforms
- **Atmosphere**: Ozone + cinnamon (burning policy = red flag), cold, SOS rhythm in node pulses
- **Mood**: Industrial danger, infrastructure crisis

### Oversight Chamber
- **Palette**: Matte black crystal, six vertical light bars (one per tongue), RU bar brightest
- **Materials**: Dark glass rear wall (reflective enough for posture as evidence), three raised desks
- **Lighting**: DR lamps make everyone look guilty including furniture
- **Mood**: Cold formal justice

## Exterior Aethermoor

### Aethermoor Overworld (from concept art + book)
- **Palette**: Purple aurora, teal crystals, gold, green, sunset pink
- **Terrain**: Floating islands (no mechanism), rope/vine bridges, crystal spires
- **Sky**: Purple aurora bands + golden energy threads, pulsing violet-gold
- **Water**: Pale blue luminescent river following invisible current (not gravity)
- **Flora**: Plants grow toward leyline light, meadows on undersides of floating masses (grass pointing DOWN)
- **Fauna**: Crystal-winged birds scattering rainbow fragments, herd animals on floating boulders, luminous dragonflies
- **Mood**: Impossible but coherent, Studio Ghibli meets JRPG

### The World Tree / Gathering Tree (from concept art)
- **Trunk**: Massive, Sacred Tongue script glowing on bark
- **Setting**: Stone arched courtyard, feast tables, lanterns, crowd gathered
- **Sky**: Aurora borealis
- **Mood**: Warm communal festival, safe haven
