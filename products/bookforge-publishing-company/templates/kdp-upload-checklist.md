# KDP Upload Checklist

## Before Upload

- Interior PDF opens.
- Cover PDF opens.
- EPUB opens for Kindle path.
- DOCX exists for backup/edit handoff.
- Title, subtitle, author, publisher, copyright, and ISBN language are correct.
- Page count is recorded.
- Cover wrap was rebuilt after final page count.
- Files use simple names with no emoji or unsupported special characters.

## KDP Upload

- Upload interior.
- Run Print Previewer.
- Record KDP-reported page count.
- If page count changed, rebuild cover wrap.
- Upload cover.
- Review previewer warnings.
- Order proof copy when appropriate.

## Do Not Claim

- Do not claim KDP approval before KDP approves it.
- Do not claim bestseller, category rank, or ad performance.
- Do not claim legal, tax, copyright, or ISBN advice.
