## Short Description

## Long Description

BookForge Publishing Kit is for authors and small publishers who want a clean
path from manuscript Markdown to KDP upload files. It includes profile presets
for common KDP trims, a starter manuscript, blurb JSON, author intake, launch
notes, and a fulfillment checklist built around the `scbe-bookforge` engine.

The engine is open source. This kit is the done-for-you wrapper: the presets,
the operational steps, and the upload discipline.

## Included

- Five profile presets.
- Starter manuscript.
- Blurb template.
- Author intake.
- KDP checklist.
- Build receipt template.
- Fulfillment SOP.
- Launch plan.
