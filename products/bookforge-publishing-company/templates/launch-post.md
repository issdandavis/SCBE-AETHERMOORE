# Launch Post

I built the BookForge Publishing Kit for authors who want Markdown to KDP files
without fighting trim sizes, cover wrap math, EPUB export, and upload checklists.

It includes:

- KDP profile presets,
- starter manuscript,
- blurb JSON,
- author intake,
- build receipt,
- upload checklist,
- fulfillment SOP.

The engine is open source. The kit is the practical wrapper that makes the
workflow turnkey.
