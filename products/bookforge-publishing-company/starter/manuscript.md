# Your Book Title

## Chapter 1. The Clean Build

This is a starter manuscript for BookForge. Replace this text with your own
book. Keep one H1 title at the top, then use H2 headings for chapters.

BookForge can build an interior PDF, a cover-wrap proof, an EPUB, and a DOCX
from one project folder. The goal is not to make authors learn layout software.
The goal is to make the upload folder repeatable.

---

Scene breaks are written as three hyphens on their own line.

## Chapter 2. The Upload Folder

Each build should produce a folder with the source profile, generated files, and
a short receipt. If KDP reports a different page count during preview, update
the `page_count` field and rebuild the cover wrap.

## Chapter 3. The Human Pass

No formatter replaces editorial judgment. Read the PDF. Open the EPUB. Check the
DOCX. Make sure the title, author, copyright, and ISBN language are correct.
