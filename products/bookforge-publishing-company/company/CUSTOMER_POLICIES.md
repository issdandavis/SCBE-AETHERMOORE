# Customer Policies

## Support Boundary

The $19 kit includes the digital files and a reasonable delivery-recovery path.
It does not include unlimited support, manuscript editing, cover design, KDP
account operation, or live screen-share setup.

## Refund Boundary

Reasonable refund window:

- Refund if the buyer cannot access the ZIP and support cannot restore access.
- Refund if the delivered ZIP is missing the promised kit files.
- Do not refund because KDP rejects a buyer-modified file after delivery.
- Do not refund because the buyer expected legal, tax, ISBN, copyediting, or ad
  services not included in the offer.

## Manual Delivery

For Ko-fi, Cash App, or manual invoice purchases:

1. Confirm payment receipt.
2. Send the delivery email template.
3. Attach or link the current ZIP.
4. Record delivery date and package hash.
5. Offer the $99 Build Audit if they want project-specific help.

## Escalation

Escalate to a paid service when the buyer asks for:

- A manuscript cleaned or converted.
- A KDP rejection diagnosed.
- Cover dimensions recalculated for a changed page count.
- File upload handholding.
- A full done-for-you build.
