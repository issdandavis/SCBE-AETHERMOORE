## Summary

Defines role-based multi-AI collaboration framework for architecture, implementation, security audit, documentation, and integration coordination.

## Operational structure

1. Role definitions by responsibility and deliverables.
2. Coordination artifacts (status context, consistency checklist, implementation tracker, public/private matrix).
3. Hand-off conventions (role tags, commit prefixes, session boundary rules).
4. Publication gate and conflict resolution protocols.

## Practical value

1. Good governance template for multi-model pipelines.
2. Strong candidate to convert into enforceable runbooks and CI gates.
3. Maps cleanly to SCBE build/document/route split.
