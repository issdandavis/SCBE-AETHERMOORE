What is AetherLand?

The place your agents actually live.

AetherLand is the virtual operating world built on top of SCBE-AETHERMOORE. It’s where governed agents, bounded desktops, CLIs, and human operators meet in a consistent, auditable space.

Think of it as a living desktop + agent ecosystem — beautiful, safe, and fully receipted.
Local-first & Bounded
Everything runs on your machine with strict guardrails. No rogue actions.
Receipts for everything
Every command, browser action, and agent step produces a signed GeoSeal receipt.
Beautiful by design
A desktop that feels alive. Windows, agents, notebooks, and tools in one cohesive land.
