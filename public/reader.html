What To Judge

Keep the emulator loop focused on whether the adaptation reads well on a phone.

Reading Pass

Read straight through and judge rhythm, pacing, and whether the story breathes like a mobile-native scroll.

  • Check reveal timing and white-space pacing.
  • Watch for abrupt slice transitions.
  • Notice where the chapter feels too dense or too empty.

Lettering Pass

Switch between text-overlay and image-only to isolate lettering problems from layout or composition problems.

  • Look for cramped dialogue and tiny captions.
  • Flag generated-text artifacts or weak emphasis.
  • Check whether visual beats survive the vertical slicing.
Ops still exists, but it is secondary. The emulator should feel like a reading device first and a control surface second.
