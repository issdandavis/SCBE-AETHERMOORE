Small Business Helper

Polly Helper is the first concrete mobile surface for the small-business product: one assistant lane for bookkeeping, customer reply drafting, inventory triage, and compliance review, with a built-in export path for future model training.

Use now

  • Draft customer replies and escalation notes.
  • Summarize inventory or storefront issues into action lists.
  • Review contracts or policies in read-only mode.
  • Compare larger frontier models against Polly in the same thread.

Training loop

  • Export thread saves the raw conversation bundle.
  • Export SFT turns assistant replies into JSONL rows for training.
  • Export feedback captures simple preference labels for later tuning.
