## Full Description
AetherBrowse is an AI-guided browser built on SCBE governance. It gives you a built-in AI sidepanel for research, planning, and web actions while enforcing policy checks before risky actions run.

Core experience:
- AI sidepanel for page summaries, action plans, and task handoffs
- Governed browser actions (navigate, extract, click/type with gate checks)
- Multi-agent loop support with auditable decision records
- Session traces and safety telemetry for reproducible workflows

Built for:
- Technical research
- Workflow automation
- Governance-first browsing in enterprise and creator environments
