# Federal Opportunity Sources

Use these sources first. Prefer official pages before secondary commentary.

## Contract Discovery
- SAM.gov Contract Opportunities: https://sam.gov/content/opportunities
- DIU Open Solicitations: https://www.diu.mil/work-with-us/open-solicitations
- AFWERX Open Topic: https://afwerx.com/divisions/ventures/open-topic/
- SBIR.gov Funding Search: https://www.sbir.gov/funding

## Market Intelligence
- USAspending Search: https://www.usaspending.gov/search
- Procurement Data Standard context: https://www.usaspending.gov/

## Acquisition Rules / Compliance
- FAR Part 12 (Commercial Products and Services): https://www.acquisition.gov/far/part-12
- Acquisition.gov home: https://www.acquisition.gov/
- SAM entity registration help: https://sam.gov/content/home

## Quick Usage Pattern
1. Find active demand signal (opportunity or open topic).
2. Map to SCBE capability component.
3. Verify contract path constraints.
4. Build a one-page capture packet.
