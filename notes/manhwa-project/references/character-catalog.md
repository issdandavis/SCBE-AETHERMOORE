- **Hair**: Short dark messy hair
- **Eyes**: Tired dark brown with dark circles
- **Face**: Angular jawline, light stubble, light olive skin
- **Build**: Lean desk-worker (not muscular)
- **Outfit (Earth)**: Dark navy hoodie over wrinkled white button-down, dark jeans. NO GLASSES.
- **Outfit (Aethermoor)**: Same Earth clothes + crystal-grip boots (from Bram)
- **Hands**: Keyboard hands — long fingers, slightly pale
- **Personality cues**: Eyes sharpen when thinking, soften when feeling. Reaches for coffee as Earth tether.
- **Visual anchor**: `gen_ch01_v3_full.py` MARCUS constant

### Izack Thorne — The Architect (Historical)

### Kael Thorne — The Spiral-Walker (Not Villain)
- **Face**: Teenager face with "grandfather patience"
- **Code signature**: Clean, recursive, self-propagating — "almost beautiful"
- **History**: Saw the gap in the system at 14 (the dead have no governance path). Weaponized by Dominion handlers by 16. Erased, not killed.
- **Sacred Egg**: Prismatic (all colors) — the synthesis tongue
- **In Book One**: Present through his code, his philosophy, and how others react to his name. Polly storm-flattens. Senna's jaw sets. Bram swears in Draumric.
- **Arc**: Redemption, not villainy. His diagnosis was correct. His architecture was wrong.

## Extended Cast (Chapters 7+)

### Lyra Thorne — Probability Researcher
- **Sacred Egg**: Emerald
- **Companion**: Quiris (spider-lizard, curiosity) — shifts on her shoulder when she's reading someone
- **Personality**: Sharp, teasing, emotionally perceptive. Materializes at the optimal moment for maximum social damage. Delivers truth disguised as banter.
- **Visual shorthand**: Emerald glint, swift arrival, the grin that means she knows something you don't
- **Key line**: "The gate is not blushing. You are."

### Mira Thorne — Verdant Tithe Keeper
- **Sacred Egg**: Sapphire
- **Companion**: Whispenook (shadow-light bat, wisdom) — hangs from her wrist, motionless enough to feel theoretical
- **Personality**: Chose silence. Forest-keeper. Level voice that says things that sound gentle until you realize they were scalpels.
- **Visual shorthand**: Forest light, unhurried movement, eyes that see through performance

### Clayborn — Golem Musician
- **Build**: Large, golem-proportioned, made of living earth
- **Instrument**: Deep-resonance instrument played against the World Tree roots
- **Effect**: When he plays, the tree's bark script changes rhythm. When the melody slows, children stop running without being told.
- **Deeper lore**: Clay is Izack's first creation — made from sand and need on a beach. "Together-story now." Proof that collaboration produces life, not just power. Clayborn in the book carries this weight without explanation. He just feels right.

### Phasewing — Senna's Companion
- **Type**: Whisper-fox, integration
- **Sacred Egg**: Opaline
- **Behavior**: Silver and opaline, fox-shaped discontinuity with opinions about floor plans. Flickers at Senna's boots.
