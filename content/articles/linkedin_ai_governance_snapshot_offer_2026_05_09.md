# AI Governance Snapshot: fixed-scope review for one AI workflow

I opened a fixed-scope AI Governance Snapshot offer for small teams, founders, and subcontract leads using AI.

The offer is simple:

- $500 fixed price
- one AI workflow, toolchain, or customer-facing AI feature
- 2-page findings memo
- three prioritized fixes
- practical evidence checklist
- no secrets or production credentials needed for the first pass

This is for teams that know they are using AI, but do not yet have a clean answer for:

- What data enters the system?
- What decisions does the AI influence?
- What gets logged?
- Where could vendor, prompt, or workflow risk show up?
- What evidence would a customer, partner, or reviewer expect?

The point is not to sell a giant compliance program. The point is to create a useful first map: what matters, what is missing, and what to fix first.
