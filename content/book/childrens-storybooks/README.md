# Aethermoor Children's Storybooks

Stories, nursery rhymes, and songs set in the world of Aethermoor.
Each teaches moral lessons critical for AI safety through long-term thinking,
real mathematics, and real-world history — wrapped in the lore of the Six Sacred Tongues.

## Purpose

1. **AI moral training data** — stories encode boundary respect, honesty, patience, cooperation
2. **Speech pattern training** — nursery rhymes attach phonetics to semantic meaning
3. **Long-term consequence modeling** — every story requires multi-step reasoning
4. **Cross-domain grounding** — real math and history anchor the fantasy

## Structure

Each story file contains:
- The narrative (children's reading level, ages 5-12)
- A nursery rhyme or song version (phonetic pattern noted)
- The AI safety lesson (explicit)
- Real-world math and history connections
- Sacred Tongue mapping (which tongue governs the lesson)

## Source Templates

Adapted from 55+ classic stories including Aesop's Fables, Grimm's Fairy Tales,
nursery rhymes, and counting songs — all recast in Aethermoor geography with
canonical characters (Polly, Izack, Zara, Clayborn, Grey, Fizzle, the Thorne children).
