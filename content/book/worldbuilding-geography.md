# Worldbuilding Geography Reference -- Spiral of Pollyoneth

Dense writer's cheat sheet. Source: "Geography and Natural Lore of the Spiral of Pollyoneth" (21-page codex).

---

## 1. AETHERMOOR (The First Realm)

The prime mortal world. Timeline spans Age of Dawn -> Age of Unity -> Modern Era. Key epochal events: Dragon Wars, Void Incursions.

### Peoples

| People | Description | Culture Notes |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| **Sylvani** | Elder elven forestfolk | Symbiotic with forest spirits. Plant a tree for every child born, linking their life force. |
| **Drakmori** | Draconic-blooded clans | Bond with drakes, take on dragon aspects. Dragon-egg hatching as coming-of-age rite. |
| **Nordgrim** | Northern barbarians | Revere giant beast totems (bears, mammoths), live alongside them. Bards memorize landscapes as songs ("Lay of Nine Valleys" = encoded directions). |
| **Sunward** | Desert clans (Sul'dessar) | Storm-forged barbarian clans, oath-bound oasis cults. Nomadic, follow ancient spirit-paths between oasis nodes. |
| **Deepstone** | Dwarves (subterranean) | Carved entire kingdoms in cavern networks. Runic Engineers guild: technomages blending magic + machinery. Created the Chronosextant. |

### Institutions

- **Grand Academy of Aethermoor** -- Traditional, conservative, hierarchical school of magic. Across the sea from Nirestal. Izack's Avalon Academy is considered radical by comparison.
- **Order of the Silver Flame** -- Arose in Aethermoor (details sparse; military/religious order implied).
- **Circle of Speakers** -- Sages who map political influence and magical currents rather than physical terrain.

## 2. NIRESTAL (Second Realm -- "The Breathed Stone")

Primary mortal continent of humans + allied races. Bounded by Crying Cliffs (north), Algrithian Coast (east), Seastone Trenches (west), burning sandstone basins (far south). Climates shift with **geomantic tides**, seasonal **aether-storms**, and **terrain memory** (residual echoes of dead civilizations embedded in the land). A **star-fall crater lake** lies at its center. Bedrock laced with ley lines that dictate where cities form.

## 3. AVALON (Dimensional Heart -- Academia Prismata)

Pocket dimension created by elf-mage **Izack**. Built on spiraling leylines. Started as a single floating island, now small-planet-scale with breathable atmosphere and magically managed climates. Philosophy: magic is a living, communicative language, not a tool. Grew in concentric rings "like tree growth" -- even expanded dramatically during Izack's absence.

### Core Geography

| Feature | Description | Sensory Hook |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **Spiral Spire** | Central tower of pale crystal stone. Absorbs starlight, reflects runic constellations at night. Rotates slowly. Each floor = a School of Magic ascending in complexity. Peak = Crown of Thought (open-air observatory). | "Hums with ambient resonance." Students hear it singing in dreams. |
| **Spiral Nexus** | Grand intersection of all major leylines. Zero-point where space-time coils densely. Beneath the academy. | Perpetual auroras overhead. Leyline spirals create micro-seasons. |
| **Leyline Gazebo** | Pavilion at precise center of Spiral Nexus. Used for meditation and delicate rituals. | Raw magical lines converge visibly. |
| **Dream Gardens** | Exotic flora from many realms. Living classrooms. Chrono-blooms, Thoughtvines grow under musical supervision. | Rune-carving beavers chew spell-patterns into trees. |
| **Hall of Harmonized Memory** | Archive in living stone. Inscriptions only appear when certain songs are played. | Stone walls ripple with hidden text when the right melody sounds. |
| **Leyline Rivers** | Semi-ethereal streams of energy + water. Flow across landscape. | Glow azure to violet. Respond to travelers' emotions. |
| **Floating Shelves of the Eastern Rise** | Chain of hovering plateaus along ley currents. Academy of wind magic built atop. | Entire mountain peaks levitate and drift. |

### Avalon Academy (Academia Prismata)

- Campus is a small city. Buildings cluster around ley nodes; paths follow energy flows, not urban grids. Organic, maze-like.
- **Navigation by attunement** -- dormitory doors only appear to those who hum a specific chord.
- Governed by the **Circle of Nine** (senior mages) in Izack's absence.
- Four outer academies align with seasons and elements.
- Marketplaces sell runic charcoal, bottled lightning. Dining halls adjust menus by eater's elemental affinity.
- Inns/taverns enchanted to soothe magical burnout.
- Protective golems patrol sky-bridges between spirettes and dormitories.
- Gravity is variable: one isle may have low gravity (giant trees), another presses down heavily (endurance training).

## 4. VARN'KA'ZUL (Demon Realm -- "The Unspoken Deep")

Also called **Malrath Varn**. Dark mirror of Avalon. Realm of logic-entangled pain and recursive evil. Governed by unbreakable contracts and soul-debts. Anti-creation: corrodes language, memory, form. Light is inverted (shadows blaze, air is dim).

### Geography (All Hostile)

| Feature | Description | Sensory Hook |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **Void-canyons / Nullfire Rivers** | Rivers of obsidian flame flow UPWARD, consuming light and gravity. | Black flame rising toward sky. |
| **Floating Fortress-Islands** | Drift mid-air, constantly colliding and rebounding in slow motion. Each ruled by a demon prince chained to a piece of their soul. | Grinding stone-on-stone as islands nudge each other. |
| **Bone Forests** | Towering bone spires that sing dissonantly whenever a lie is told nearby. | Discordant wailing when deception occurs. |
| **Ash Volcanoes** | Erupt ash forming screaming faces; lava congeals into grasping hands. | Landscape tries to physically drag intruders down. |
| **Infernal Glasslands** | Plains of black obsidian glass formed by hellfire. Home to memory-wasps. | Ground reflects a twisted version of sky + your worst failure. |
| **Ashen Marshes** | "Water" is liquid despair. | -- |
| **Reversal Mire** | Swamp that inverts life/death: living beings become momentarily undead; corpses animate. | Your heart stops, thoughts twist. Skeletons wade upright. |
| **Shredding Thicket** | Bramble that attacks the mind directly, stripping away memories. | Dark reflection of Avalon's Mind-Forest. |
| **Null Zones** | Utterly devoid of creation. Nothing grows. | Total emptiness. |

### Key Demon Settlement

- **Zul-Qa'ath, Citadel of Eternal Contract** -- City of devils built into a canyon wall. Every edifice inscribed with binding runes. Labyrinth of bridges over a bottomless pit. Souls of the damned shuffle in chains. Antagonist's base.

### Demon Fauna

- Obsidian ravens (dark Polly reflections, carry lies on wings)
- Shadow-hounds (hunt fear, not flesh)
- Memory-wasps (inject false memories instead of venom)
- Flamebinders (apex predator demons)
- Imps (pest-tier)
- Gear-spirits / clock-blooded beasts (twisted constructs, endlessly repetitive)
- Ashlotus flowers (bloom in despair-pools, explode into choking ash if touched)

## 5. DIVINE & ELEMENTAL PLANES

- **Celestial / Primordial Realm** -- Preceded Avalon. Source of raw unshaped magic and cosmic resonance. Myth-level.
- **Four Elemental Kingdoms** (Earth, Air, Fire, Water) -- Adjacent to mortal plane. Domains of elemental spirits and minor gods. Visited via summoning rituals or astral projection. Influence the world subtly (unseasonable storms, quakes from elemental rifts).
- **Four Elemental Temples** scattered across the world:
  - Temple at volcanic crater (south desert) -- houses the **Salamander Throne** where a Fire Genie taught secrets of flame.
  - Temple atop mountain peak in perpetual storm.
  - Temple under a great waterfall.
  - Temple in crystal cavern underground.
  - Each guarded by elemental spirits; contains portal to its Elemental Kingdom.

### Inter-Realm Spaces

| Location | Type | Details |

## 6. TRAVEL ROUTES AND TRANSIT SYSTEMS

### Mundane Travel

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **King's Highways** | Imperial roads with waystones every league. At dusk, waystones glow; in danger, project warning illusions (lion's roar). |
| **Bridge-Golems** | Unfurl themselves to span rivers on command. |
| **Toll Spirits** | Demand a spoken story or riddle as payment at ancient bridges. |
| **Horse/Wagon/Ship** | Standard. |
| **Airships** | Rare, expensive. Captured wind elementals bound to sails. Connect far-flung regions in days vs. weeks. Dwarf-tinker + Avalon enchanter collaboration. |
| **Skybeasts (Avalon only)** | Giant dragonfly mounts, gentle sky rays (stingray-like, swims through air). Bred from "cloudseed pods" Izack developed. Student transport between floating isles. |
| **Stormwind** | Izack's horse. Mane crackles with static runes, senses temporal anomalies, navigates paradox storms. Empathic; runic hoofprints detect traps. |

### Magical Transit

| Method | Mechanism | Risk |
|--------|-----------|------|
| **Fold-Ways** | Secret paths via folded space. Archways/standing stones as doorways. Need a tuning crystal attuned to path frequency. Originally laid by elven Dimensional Weavers. | Get lost in the in-between without proper crystal/sigil. |
| **Echo Tunnels** | Sound precedes physical presence through cave/canyon overlaps in space-time. Bards/echo-mages amplify into usable portals. Notable: Whisperdelve caverns under Ravencrest. | Dangerous, unpredictable. Echo-demons lurk, imitating friend voices. |
| **Time-Bridges** | Paths through time, not space. Appear during eclipses/conjunctions. Shimmering causeway of light. Hourglass Bridge on Avalon campus = 24hr jump. | Paradoxes. Heavy narrative restrictions. |
| **Ley Rides** | Merge aura with ley stream. Dematerialize into energy flow, ride to junction. Ancient druids sent groups across continents in minutes. | Strong will required or be ripped apart. Unfocused travelers lost when line dives underground. |
| **Portal Gates / Spellgates** | Fixed stone arches/doorframes. Keystones or coded spells activate. Avalon has several on periphery. One-use emergency scroll/crystal portals also exist. | Misdrawn rune = void or wrong realm. |
| **World Tree Bridge** | Ritual at tree base in Ravencrest garden -> enveloped by roots -> emerge from corresponding tree at Avalon's Leyline Gazebo. | Serene but humbling. Travelers see flashes of ancient memories, feel weight of ages. |

### Travel Hazards

- **Rift Storms** -- Wild magic pockets that appear on roads. Can displace travelers in space or time.
- **Echo-Demons** -- Minor entities in echo tunnels/fold-ways, mimic friend voices to lure travelers astray.
- **Paradox Storms** -- Chaotic time currents (Stormwind the horse specializes in navigating these).

### Roadside Infrastructure

- **Wandering Willow Inn** (Verdant Tithe crossroads) -- Dryad-blessed, no aggressive magic possible on premises. Neutral ground.
- **Milestone Shrines** (Imperial High Road) -- Carved guardian faces ward off night creatures.

## 11. CARTOGRAPHIC MYSTICISM

Maps in this world are interactive, sometimes sentient, and can affect reality.

### Key Cartographic Artifacts

| Artifact | Function |
|----------|----------|
| **Sentient Atlas** | Bound to Avalon. Blank until a traveler speaks of their journey; pages fill in. Rejects lies (ink bleeds, rearranges). |
| **Skin Map of Zal'gor** | Tattooed on preserved skin of ancient cartographer. Shifts to show current demon territory control. Shows false routes to those who intend harm. |
| **Mirror Atlas** | Grand Academy vault. Shows map based on observer's personal memory. Different viewers see different versions. Powerful mages could theoretically reshape reality through it. |
| **Star Chart of Aethermoor** | Only reveals hidden fortress location under certain constellations + incantation. |
| **Marley's Living Maps** | Moss + crystals on sand tray. Pour water (= ley flow), hum a tune -> moss grows into miniature moving topographical map. Shows Ent movement, evil influence spreading. |

### Cartographic Rules

- **"To map a place is to impose understanding upon it."** Marley's precisely drawn path through a bramble maze caused the real brambles to part.
- Requires special inks (blood or enchanted dyes) and knowledge of a place's "true name."
- Malicious use: erasing a village from a map could cause it to vanish or be forgotten.
- Demon Realm maps require ash + blood ink; always show false routes to those who'd harm the drawer.
- Avalon's shifting nature means maps older than months are outdated unless magically updated.
- Nordgrim encode directions in songs ("Lay of Nine Valleys" = melodic map).
- Geographic Overlays: transparent maps layered (ley lines over topography over population) to identify strategic sites.
- Cartographer tools: compass-roses that spin near illusions, auto-scaling quills, enchanted scout-birds.

### Named Cartographer

- **Marley** -- Field cartographer, companion to Izack. Pioneer of living-map techniques. Frontier town Marley's Rest named after him.

## 12. ENVIRONMENTAL MAGIC RULES

Core principle: **"Magic is environmental memory."** The land remembers what happened. Great acts of healing make valleys permanently greener; battlefields remain scarred.

| Rule | Manifestation |
|------|---------------|
| Terrain memory | Old battlefields make blood-red clay. Plains of Echoes replay phantom armies at dawn. |
| Glacial memory-ice | Touching it shows the frozen tragedy within. |
| Emotional resonance | Singing Dunes judge hearts: joyful hear comfort, guilty hear wails. |
| Anti-divination zones | Dremsreach mud blocks scrying/foresight. |
| Ley influence on settlement | Towns near stable leylines get better crops, milder weather. |
| Magic surges | Wild mana spring can mutate bear into multi-headed chaos beast. |
| Life generates magic | Large gatherings of creatures or ancient trees create their own leylines. |
| Predator aura tracking | Magical beasts track wizard "spellprints." |
| Magical camouflage | Prey species blink out of reality (chameleonetts) or evolve other defenses. |
| Cross-realm contamination | Moving ecosystems between realms requires runic soil anchors and buffer curtains. Each region's magic is like a language dialect. |
