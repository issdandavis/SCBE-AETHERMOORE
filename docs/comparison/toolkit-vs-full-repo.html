The fastest way to disappoint a buyer is to make them guess what they purchased. This page exists to reduce mismatch: toolkit = starter surface with one delivery path; repo = wide surface with many lanes.

Category Toolkit (what you buy) Full repo (what exists)
Buyer path Manual-first delivery, access steps, recovery route. Wide surface; you can get lost without a map.
Governance patterns Starter patterns, templates, and examples you can adopt quickly. Broader research, experiments, and evolving components.
Proof surfaces Curated proof pages aligned to buyer questions. Deeper technical material, code, and exploratory docs.
Support Direct route if delivery is missing/broken or you hit a setup blocker. Not a guarantee of ongoing bespoke consulting.
Goal Start governed AI work without digging through the whole repo. Explore the full SCBE system when you are ready.

Buyers who should start with the toolkit

If your goal is to govern a workflow fast and you need a clear delivery path.

  • You are deploying LLM features into real environments.
  • You need a smaller surface to adopt before expanding.
  • You want proof pages and manuals, not just code.

Builders who will also want the repo later

If your goal is to extend the system and you want the full architecture and research surface.

  • You are building agentic infrastructure and want deeper internals.
  • You want to inspect the research notes and training lanes.
  • You are comfortable with breadth and evolving components.
