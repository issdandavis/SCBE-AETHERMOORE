What support does

  • Keeps the SCBE-AETHERMOORE open-source stack moving.
  • Funds cleanup, packaging, delivery fixes, and benchmark runs.
  • Supports decision record templates, threshold notes, and pilot checklist work.
  • Helps turn review notes into reusable docs, manuals, and delivery pages.
  • Creates a low-friction path before formal consulting or procurement.
  • Supports the free public pages people can inspect before checkout.

Good fit / not for

Good fit: people who want the system to keep getting built and want practical notes without waiting for a polished course, enterprise onboarding, or a sales call.

Not for: emergency support, custom development, legal/compliance certification, or a guaranteed service level from a small support payment.

Success check

A useful support lane should make the public stack better: cleaner docs, clearer delivery, stronger tests, easier payment paths, and less friction for the next buyer.

Is support a product purchase?

No. Support helps keep the work moving. If you need a specific deliverable, use the Workflow Snapshot, hosted-run intake, service credits, or products page.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Stripe monthly support can be cancelled through Stripe. Ko-fi and Cash App can be used for one-time support with no subscription.
