1. Context (2 min)
- “What process is currently costing your team the most time each week?”

2. Pain and impact (4 min)
- “How often does it happen?”
- “Who touches it today?”
- “What breaks most often?”

3. Feasibility and constraints (4 min)
- “Which systems are involved (Shopify, Gmail, docs, API)?”
- “Do you need human approval gates before final actions?”

4. Offer fit (3 min)
