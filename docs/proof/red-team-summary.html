Red-team summary

SCBE review looks for practical failure modes in an AI workflow, then turns them into fixes a team can actually apply.

  • Input risk: where unsafe, private, or low-quality data enters the workflow.
  • Tool risk: which commands, files, network calls, or payments the AI can touch.
  • Decision risk: where a human needs to approve, deny, or preserve evidence.
  • Recovery risk: what happens when the system is wrong, overloaded, or interrupted.
