Why the manual exists

SCBE products are not only files. They are small governed workflows. The manual exists so a buyer can inspect the package, understand delivery, and know where support starts before committing to a larger engagement.

  • Before checkout: the public manual explains the scope, delivery path, and support route.
  • After checkout: the buyer gets immediate instructions instead of waiting for a sales call.
  • During review: the manual becomes the decision record for what was included, excluded, and handled later.
