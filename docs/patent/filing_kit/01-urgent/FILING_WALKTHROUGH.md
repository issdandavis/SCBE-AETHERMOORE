# USPTO Filing Walkthrough — Missing Parts for #63/961,403

**Time needed**: ~30 minutes
**Cost**: $65 base provisional micro-entity filing fee (verify cart total at checkout)
**Deadline**: April 19, 2026

---

## BEFORE YOU START

You need:
- [ ] Computer with internet
- [ ] Credit or debit card (expected base fee: $65)
- [ ] Your PTO/SB/15A filled out and saved as PDF (see PTO_SB_15A_MICRO_ENTITY_CERT.md)
- [ ] Your PTO/SB/16 filled out and saved as PDF (see PTO_SB_16_COVER_SHEET.md)
- [ ] Your USPTO account login (or create one below)

---

## STEP 1 — Get to Patent Center

1. Open your browser
2. Go to: **https://patentcenter.uspto.gov**
3. Click **"Sign In"** (top right)

### If you DON'T have a USPTO account yet:

1. Click **"Create Account"** on the login page
2. You'll be sent to MyUSPTO registration
3. Fill in your name, email, create password
4. Verify your email
5. Come back to Patent Center and sign in

### If you already have one:

1. Enter your email and password
2. Sign in

---

## STEP 2 — Find Your Application

1. After signing in, you'll see the Patent Center dashboard
2. Look for **"Existing Application"** or **"Manage Applications"**
3. Enter your application number: **63/961,403**
4. Click **Search** or **Retrieve**
5. Your application "Context-Bound Cryptographic Authorization System" should appear
6. Click on it to open it

---

## STEP 3 — Respond to the Missing Parts Notice

1. In your application view, look for **"Submissions"** or **"File a Document"**
   or **"Follow-on Submissions"**
2. Click to start a new submission
3. You'll see a form asking what type of document you're filing

### Select document type:
- Choose **"Response to Notice to File Missing Parts"** or similar
- If that exact option isn't there, choose **"Other"** and type
  "Response to Notice to File Missing Parts of Application"

---

## STEP 4 — Upload Your Documents

1. You'll see an **"Upload Documents"** or **"Attach Files"** section
2. Upload these files (PDF format):

| File | Description to Enter |
|------|---------------------|
| PTO_SB_15A.pdf | Certification of Micro Entity Status |
| PTO_SB_16.pdf | Provisional Application Cover Sheet |

3. For each file, you may need to select a **"Document Category"**:
   - PTO/SB/15A → Category: "Petition" or "Certification"
   - PTO/SB/16 → Category: "Application Data Sheet" or "Cover Sheet"
4. If neither category is available, use "Other" and type the form name

---

## STEP 5 — Pay the Filing Fee

1. After uploading documents, you'll be taken to a **"Fees"** section
2. The system should show the provisional application filing fee
3. Verify the amount: **$65.00** (current base Micro Entity provisional filing fee)
   - If it shows $130 (Small Entity) or $325 (Undiscounted), STOP
   - Make sure your PTO/SB/15A was uploaded correctly
   - You may need to select "Micro Entity" in the entity status dropdown
   - If the total is higher than $65 due to an added surcharge line item, review the fee breakdown before paying
4. Enter your credit/debit card information
5. Click **"Submit Payment"** or **"Pay"**

---

## STEP 6 — Confirm and Save

1. You'll get a **confirmation page** with a receipt number
2. **SAVE THIS PAGE** — screenshot it, print to PDF, whatever works
3. Save the receipt number somewhere safe
4. You should also receive a confirmation email

### What to save:
- [ ] Confirmation/receipt number
- [ ] Date of submission
- [ ] Amount paid (expected base fee: $65, plus any system-calculated surcharge)
- [ ] Screenshot or PDF of confirmation page

---

## STEP 7 — Verify (next day)

1. Log back into Patent Center the next business day
2. Pull up application 63/961,403
3. Check that your submission shows as "Received" or "Filed"
4. Check that the Missing Parts notice shows as "Responded"

---

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### "I can't find my application"
- Double-check the number: 63/961,403
- Try with and without the slash
- If it was filed January 15, 2026, it should be there
- Contact USPTO customer service: 1-800-786-9199

### "The fee is showing wrong amount"
- Make sure you selected "Micro Entity" as your entity status
- Make sure PTO/SB/15A was uploaded
- If the system doesn't reflect micro entity, you can:
  - Call USPTO and ask them to update entity status
  - Or pay the higher amount and file a petition to refund the difference later

### "I don't see a 'Missing Parts' response option"
- Try: Submissions → New Submission → select your application
- Or try: "File a follow-on document"
- Patent Center UI changes occasionally — look for any option to
  "add documents" or "respond" to your application

### "My account won't let me access the application"
- You need to be the applicant/inventor on record
- The name and email must match what was filed
- If you filed through a different method (mail, EFS-Web), you may need
  to link the application to your Patent Center account

---

## AFTER FILING — WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

1. USPTO processes your response (usually 1-2 weeks)
2. Your provisional application is now complete with priority date Jan 15, 2026
3. You have until **January 15, 2027** to convert to a non-provisional (~$400)
4. In the meantime, you can file additional provisionals for new inventions (currently $65 each at Micro Entity base rate)
