## People Who Can Help (Official Lanes)

### 1) Registered Patent Attorneys/Agents (Paid)
- Use USPTO OED practitioner search to identify registered patent attorneys/agents by location and reg number.
- Source: https://oedci.uspto.gov/OEDCI/practitionerSearchEntry

### 2) USPTO Patent Pro Bono (Cost-Reduced / Free if Qualified)
- Regional patent pro bono programs connect financially under-resourced inventors with volunteer counsel.
- Source: https://www.uspto.gov/patents/basics/using-legal-services/pro-bono/patent-pro-bono-program

### 3) Law School Clinic Certification Program
- Participating law schools can assist with patent prep/prosecution under faculty supervision.
- Source: https://www.uspto.gov/patents/basics/using-legal-services/law-school-clinic-1

### 4) USPTO Patent Pro Se Assistance Program
- For process support if you self-file any portion while counsel is being retained.
- Source: https://www.uspto.gov/patents/basics/apply/pro-se-assistance-program

### 5) PTAB Pro Bono (If Appeals Are Needed Later)
- Appeal-stage assistance pathway if prosecution reaches PTAB.
- Source: https://www.uspto.gov/patents/ptab/pro-bono-program
