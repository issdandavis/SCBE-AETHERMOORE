Working project folder for the cultivation ensemble novel.

- `canon/fenshui-zong.md` - Watershed Sect history, fall, ruins, tournament, and inner sanctum.
- `canon/chen-dashi.md` - rebuilt Chen Dashi character/family interior.
- `canon/genre-differentiation.md` - comparison pressure against core comps and unresolved risks.
- `research/author-philosophy-and-sect-positioning.md` - author craft rules, sect creation model, positioning map, and humor lane.
- `research/audience-cultural-humor-research.md` - Asian and Asian-diaspora media research, humor mechanics, idiom patterns, and Watershed adaptation rules.
- `research/combat-by-medium.md` - novel combat approach versus manhwa, anime, games, and live action.
- `research/qi-and-combat-engine.md` - Qi research, Watershed interpretation, and combat-engine mapping.
- `research/qi-in-lit-anime-manhwa.md` - genre research across cultivation novels, anime power systems, and murim manhwa.
- `scenes/book1-water-looked-embarrassed.md` - early ruins scene demonstrating Yun Zhou's non-cultivation tendency-sense.

## Current Craft Rule

Do not force every object to become meaning at the moment it appears.

Let practical life happen first. A father teaches because he is a father. A
hand wrap can just be a hand wrap. Meaning can arrive late, partially, or not
at all.
