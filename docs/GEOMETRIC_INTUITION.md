In normal space:
- Distance is symmetric
- Movement cost is uniform

In SCBE space:
- Movement cost depends on *which dimension* you move in
- And *what phase* you are in

```
Cost(x → x+δ) ≠ Cost(x+δ → x)
```

That alone breaks Euclidean intuition.

## 4. Risk is a Curvature Operator, Not a Dimension

Risk is not a direction you move in. Risk **bends the space itself**.

```
H(d) = R^(d²)
```

This means:
- Small deviations: cheap (water)
- Medium deviations: stiff (molasses)
- Large deviations: infinite (stone)

Same coordinates. Different physics.
