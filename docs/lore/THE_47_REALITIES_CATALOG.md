# The 47 Realities — Full Canon Catalog

## Mathematical Foundation

The 47 Realities are not arbitrary. They are the complete non-redundant
interaction space of the 6 Sacred Tongues:

- 6 real tongue weights (singles): KO, AV, RU, CA, UM, DR
- 15 pairwise imaginary rotations: C(6,2) = 15
- 20 three-way imaginary rotations: C(6,3) = 20
- 6 self-imaginary potentials: tongue × i = unrealized potential

Total: 6 + 15 + 20 + 6 = 47

The Sundering shattered this 47D unified space into 47 separate 1D
projections. Each reality experiences one dimension. The Crystal Helix
Chamber reconnects them.

## Spiral Structure

- **Inner Spiral (1-15)**: Stable anchors closest to Avalon's ethos
- **Mid-Spiral (16-35)**: Dynamic frontiers where diversity sparks innovation
- **Outer/Shadow Turns (36-47)**: Volatile high-drift zones
