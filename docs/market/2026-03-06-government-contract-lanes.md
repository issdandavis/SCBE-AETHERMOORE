## SLAM2ER Consortium (Expanded)
- Opportunity: NSWC PCD RFI `N61331-26-9-0001` for a single Consortium Management firm under OTA authorities (10 U.S.C. 4021/4022).
- Role sought: consortium manager/operator, not just technology performer.
- Mission domain: seabed/subsea, littoral/coastal defense, asymmetric-autonomous systems, mine and mine-hunting, expeditionary robotics.
- Technical scope includes C5ISRT and adjacent naval autonomy/cyber stacks; projects may include classified and unclassified work.
- Key submission constraints:
- white paper PDF, 10 pages max (12-point Times New Roman),
- no classified material in response,
- explicit process descriptions for membership, evaluation, fees/dues, financial controls, and unilateral-change handling.
- Key dates (as listed in the RFI text mirror):
- questions due: March 6, 2026 at 12:00 PM CST,
- responses due: March 13, 2026 at 12:00 PM CST.
- Go-to-market implication for SCBE:
- strongest near-term play is subcontract/teaming with a consortium manager candidate,
- secondary play is direct prototype responses once downstream calls release under the OTA framework.

## DARPA AI Safety Program Map (Actionable)
1. CLARA (Compositional Learning-And-Reasoning for AI Complex Systems Engineering)
- Focus: high-assurance AI by tightly integrating automated reasoning and ML.
- Relevance to SCBE: direct fit for verifiability, compositional governance, and explainable control layers.
- Status signal: DARPA lists a 2026 disruption opportunity with solicitation and session references.

2. AIxCC (AI Cyber Challenge)
- Focus: AI systems that find/patch software vulnerabilities at scale for critical infrastructure.
- Relevance to SCBE: strong adjacency for hardening autonomous stack software and secure fleet runtime.
- Status signal: challenge cycle launched in 2023 with finals at DEF CON 2025; now useful as transition evidence and partner discovery lane.

3. ANSR (Assured Neuro Symbolic Learning and Reasoning)
- Focus: trustworthy hybrid neuro-symbolic autonomy with robustness + assurance.
- Relevance to SCBE: conceptual overlap with semantic mesh governance and safety-constrained autonomy.
- Status signal: program framing remains important for positioning even where the initial solicitation cycle is older.
