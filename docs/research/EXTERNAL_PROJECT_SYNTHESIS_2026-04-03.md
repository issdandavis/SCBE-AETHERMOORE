## 1. Unified Canon

### Core Premise
Izack Thorne, an elven dimensional theorist, awakens after a catastrophic experiment. Guided by Polly (polydimensional raven), they claim the Chronological Nexus Staff and arrive at Ravencrest Manor. Izack bonds with Aria Ravencrest over the World Tree sapling. Their collaborative magic births Pollyoneth/Avalon -- a living, conscious academy-realm that evolves into a nexus for interdimensional education and consciousness integration.
