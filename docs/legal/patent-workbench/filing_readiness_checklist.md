## Priority Handling Rule

The non-provisional may include improvements made after the provisional filing
date, but those improvements should be labeled during drafting. Material
supported by the provisional can seek the `2026-01-15` priority date. Material
first added after that date may only receive the non-provisional filing date
unless another priority source supports it. Do not rely on post-provisional
features as if they were automatically covered by `63/961,403`.
