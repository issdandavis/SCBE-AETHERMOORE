AetherMoore is built around local-first and privacy-by-default operation. Hosted services should collect only what is needed to answer requests, deliver products, protect the service, and improve support.

Data is used to operate the website, process purchases, provide support, route AI responses, detect abuse, and improve product reliability. Sensitive secrets should not be pasted into public demos or support forms.
