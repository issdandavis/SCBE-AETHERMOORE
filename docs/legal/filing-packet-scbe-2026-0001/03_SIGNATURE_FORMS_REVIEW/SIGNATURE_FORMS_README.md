# Signature Forms Review

These files are prepared for final human review before upload to Patent Center.

## Files

- `PTO_SB_15A_micro_entity_PREFILL_REVIEW_UNSIGNED.pdf`
  - Official USPTO micro-entity certification form, gross-income basis.
  - Prefilled with inventor name and invention title only.
  - Signature, date, telephone, application number, and patent number are intentionally blank.

- `PTO_AIA_01_declaration_PREFILL_REVIEW_UNSIGNED.pdf`
  - Official USPTO inventor declaration form for an application using an ADS.
  - Prefilled with invention title and inventor name.
  - The "attached application" option is selected.
  - Signature and date are intentionally blank.

## Before Upload

1. Review the micro-entity requirements on `PTO_SB_15A`.
2. Use your tax forms only to confirm the gross-income certification is true.
3. Do not upload tax returns or private tax records unless USPTO specifically requests them.
4. Personally sign and date both forms.
5. Add your telephone number on the micro-entity form.
6. Upload the signed `PTO_SB_15A` as a micro-entity certification.
7. Upload the signed `PTO_AIA_01` as the inventor oath/declaration.

An acceptable USPTO electronic S-signature is typically formatted as `/Issac Daniel Davis/`, but the inventor must personally apply it.
