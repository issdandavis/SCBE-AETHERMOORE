## CRITICAL PATH (Do These First — They Have Lead Times)

### Step 1: Get a UEI (Unique Entity Identifier)
- **What**: Replaced DUNS number in 2022. Required for ALL federal contracting.
- **Where**: https://sam.gov/content/entity-registration
- **Time**: Typically issued within 1-2 business days, but validation can take up to 10 business days
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Action**:
  1. Go to SAM.gov
  2. Click "Get Started" under Entity Registration
  3. Select "I want to register my entity to do business with the U.S. Government"
  4. You will be asked for:
     - Legal business name (use your full legal name: Issac Daniel Davis)
     - Physical address (Port Angeles, WA)
     - Entity type: Individual / Sole Proprietor
     - EIN or SSN (for sole proprietors, SSN is acceptable)
  5. The system will assign a UEI immediately or within 1-2 days
- **STATUS**: [ ] NOT STARTED

### Step 2: Complete SAM.gov Entity Registration
- **What**: Full registration in the System for Award Management
- **Where**: https://sam.gov
- **Time**: Can take 7-10 business days for IRS validation + CAGE code assignment
- **Cost**: FREE (never pay a third party to do this)
- **Requires**: UEI (from Step 1), bank account for EFT, NAICS codes
- **Action**:
  1. Log into SAM.gov with Login.gov credentials (or create Login.gov account)
  2. Complete all sections of entity registration:
     - Core Data (legal name, address, entity type)
     - Assertions (size standard, business types)
     - Representations and Certifications
     - Points of Contact (you are all POCs as sole proprietor)
     - Financial Information (bank routing + account for direct deposit)
  3. Submit registration
  4. IRS validates your TIN — this is the slow part (up to 10 days)
  5. Once validated, a CAGE code is automatically assigned
- **IMPORTANT**: Start this IMMEDIATELY. If IRS validation takes the full 10 days, that's April 7 — cutting it close for April 17 deadline.
- **STATUS**: [ ] NOT STARTED

### Step 3: CAGE Code (Automatic with SAM.gov)
- **What**: Commercial and Government Entity code. Assigned by DLA (Defense Logistics Agency).
- **How**: Automatically generated when SAM.gov registration completes
- **Time**: Included in SAM.gov processing time (7-10 days)
- **No separate application needed**
- **STATUS**: [ ] PENDING (assigned during SAM.gov registration)
