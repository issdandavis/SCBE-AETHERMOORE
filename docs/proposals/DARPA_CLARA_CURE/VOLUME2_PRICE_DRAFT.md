## Cost Narrative

The proposed budget supports compositional ML+AR deliverables: standardized evaluation, verifiable invariant artifacts, defeasible rule exports, bounded unfolding traces, and IV&V-ready reproducibility materials.

Phase 1 focuses on inference-time composition, proof/trace export, and deterministic benchmark evidence. Phase 2 adds limited training-time adaptation, artifact preservation, rollback, multi-domain adaptation, and trace interpretability.
