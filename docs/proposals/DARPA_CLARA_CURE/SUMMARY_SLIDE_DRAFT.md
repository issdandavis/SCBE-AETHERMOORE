## Problem

Current AI safety systems usually attach automated reasoning components around machine-learning systems after the fact. Those systems can block known attacks, but they do not provide compositional assurance, proof-carrying explanations, or reliable behavior under novel attack forms.

## Technical Idea

SCBE-AETHERMOORE places automated reasoning inside the inference pipeline through a compositional ML+AR architecture:

- defeasible logic-program governance for policy decisions
- concept-bottleneck state for explainability
- geometric intent verification for bounded adversarial drift
- typed composition layers from ingestion through telemetry
- machine-checkable receipts for traceability and rollback

## CLARA Fit

The work targets CLARA's core aim: tightly integrating ML and AR components into high-assurance AI that supports automated logical proof, hierarchical explainability, and compositional reuse.
