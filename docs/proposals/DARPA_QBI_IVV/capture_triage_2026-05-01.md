## Fit Assessment

This is worth tracking, but it is not a natural direct-prime bid for SCBE as a quantum hardware IV&V provider.

The strongest SCBE fit is a narrow software and evaluation-support lane:

- independent benchmark workflow management
- audit logs and evidence packaging
- model and agent governance gates for evaluation tooling
- reproducible test harnesses
- cross-lab result packaging and review workflows
- security-conscious automation around validation artifacts
- policy-aware agent coordination for long-running review tasks

The weak fit is physical quantum validation:

- no in-house quantum hardware lab
- no cryogenic / photonic / trapped-ion test infrastructure
- no established quantum-device calibration team
- no obvious ability to independently validate performer hardware claims alone

## Recommended Route

Do not frame SCBE as the main IV&V lab.

Frame SCBE as a **software IV&V support component** that can help an IV&V prime or lab:

1. collect and normalize benchmark evidence,
2. enforce reproducible review gates,
3. track independent test artifacts,
4. generate audit-ready comparison packets,
5. coordinate multi-agent review lanes without uncontrolled execution,
6. reduce reporting and traceability burden.

Best route:

- APEX review first for federal positioning and teaming language.
- Identify likely IV&V primes, labs, or university teams.
- Prepare a one-page capability note: "SCBE-AETHERMOORE for audit-ready quantum benchmarking workflows."
- Avoid claiming quantum hardware expertise beyond what is directly evidenced.
