Delivery + Access

Shortest path when a purchase needs recovery.

Use this page when the package link, manual link, receipt, or access note is missing or broken. The product is not complete unless the buyer packet, manual, and support route are all reachable.

Delivery recovery flow

1. ReceiptSave the Stripe receipt email.
2. ProductConfirm the product name on the receipt.
3. ManualOpen the matching product manual.
4. PackageCheck that expected files are present.
5. EscalateEmail exact details if blocked.

Non-open-source delivery check

Paid ZIP artifacts are generated locally by python scripts/package_products.py and kept out of public GitHub Pages under products/packaged/. The checkout backend supports buyer-only package URLs through SCBE_TOOLKIT_DOWNLOAD_URL and SCBE_TRAINING_VAULT_DOWNLOAD_URL; those should be set before paid traffic is promoted.

What to include if delivery fails

  • Product name from the receipt.
  • Purchase email used at checkout.
  • Receipt or payment reference.
  • Exact broken URL, missing file, or error text.
  • Whether you bought the Toolkit, the Training Vault, or both.
