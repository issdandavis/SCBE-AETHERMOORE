## 1. EU AI Act Urgency -- The August 2, 2026 Deadline

### What Happens on August 2, 2026

Full enforcement begins for **Annex III high-risk AI systems**. Companies must have completed conformity assessments, finalized technical documentation, affixed CE marking, and registered in the EU database. ([Source](https://ai2.work/economics/eu-ai-act-high-risk-rules-hit-august-2026-your-compliance-countdown/))

### Penalty Exposure

| Violation Type | Maximum Fine |
|---|---|
| Prohibited AI practices | EUR 35 million or **7% of global annual turnover** |
| High-risk non-compliance | EUR 15 million or **3% of global annual turnover** |
| Supplying incorrect information | EUR 7.5 million or **1% of global annual turnover** |

Source: [EU AI Act Summary](https://www.softwareimprovementgroup.com/blog/eu-ai-act-summary/)

Per [Annex III](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/annex/3/), these sectors face mandatory compliance:

([IAPP](https://iapp.org/news/a/european-commission-misses-deadline-for-ai-act-guidance-on-high-risk-systems))
