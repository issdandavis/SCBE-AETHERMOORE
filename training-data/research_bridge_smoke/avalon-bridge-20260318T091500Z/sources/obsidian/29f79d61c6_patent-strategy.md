## Main calendar targets

USPTO guidance says a provisional application has a 12-month pendency period and does not mature into a patent by itself.

For this filing, the main target date is:

- **January 15, 2027** - target date to file a corresponding nonprovisional application or otherwise execute the next formal priority step
