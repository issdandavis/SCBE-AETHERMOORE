# Cast Bible

Writer-facing cast sort for `The Six Tongues Protocol` and the wider Pollyoneth / Avalon corpus.

This is not a purity document. It is a practical sorting tool so the live isekai manuscript stops mixing:

- `locked current-book canon`
- `wider world canon not yet on page`
- `legacy / alternate draft branches`
- `one-scene or flexible reuse names`

- `Locked current-book canon`
  The live isekai can safely use these now.

## Use Rules

- Do not promote a cool old name into canon just because it sounds good.
- Before importing a branch character, ask:
  - what thematic job do they do?
  - is that job already being done by someone on-page?
  - does the name belong to this timeline branch, or only to older lore?
- The live book should stay emotionally legible before it becomes encyclopedic.

## Best Practical Reading Of The Corpus

- The current isekai has a solid core.
- The wider Avalon lore is deep enough to feed expansion for years.
- The real danger is not lack of material.
- The real danger is branch contamination.
- Current external-pressure read:
  - the Dominion is an outside enemy — strategic, patient, exploiting real vulnerabilities
  - Kael's grievances were real — the dead backlog, the governance gap, the system's inability to acknowledge loss
  - those grievances were used as an attack vector — planted handler-friends over 2 years
  - the handlers aimed Kael's brilliance at the system's load-bearing walls, then disappeared
  - the danger is centralization, not merely rebellion — centralization dissolves the distributed keys
  - the seventh silence (domain for endings/transitions) is the real fix, not a seventh tongue

Use this note to keep those branches separated on purpose.
