# Character Research - Core Cast

## Canon Confidence Levels
- LOCKED = confirmed across 2+ sources, user-patched
- STABLE = consistent across variants, not yet locked
- PROPOSED = single-source or branch-specific

## Core Characters

### Izack Thorne (STABLE)
- **Role:** Dimensional theorist/architect, founder of Avalon
- **Displacement:** Experiment failure -> anomalous shore (T0)
- **Key artifacts:** Chronological Nexus Staff, Transdimensional Reality Robes
- **Partner:** Polly (raven, co-equal), Aria Ravencrest (spouse, co-founder)
- **Children:** Alexander Thorne (prodigy, born ~age 124)
- **Arc:** T0 displacement -> cave encounter -> Ravencrest contact -> Avalon founding -> Great Sacrifice (scatters Avalon)
- **Death:** Anchors dimensional network using Robes, becomes living bridge
- **Cross-ref:** [[Sacred Tongues - Emotional Architecture]], [[14-Layer Architecture]]

### Aria Ravencrest (LOCKED parent = Aldric)
- **Role:** Temporal mage, boundary specialist, co-founder, "Mother of Avalon"
- **House:** Ravencrest (LOCKED per canon patch 2026-02-23)
- **Father:** Count Aldric Ravencrest (studious, good-natured, lost wife to magical blight)
- **Species:** Human (Noble) vs Elf UNRESOLVED
- **Magic:** Boundary (Ward), Temporal; "boundaries are negotiations, not walls"
- **First appearance:** Chapter 3 ("Of Gardens and Gateways")
- **Marriage:** ~Izack age 123, Time-Stone Ring forged through shared boundary magic
- **Voice:** Direct, measured, protective, precise. Threshold language.
- **Weakness:** Over-rigidity under fear; fear of losing protected ones -> hard containment
- **Cross-ref:** [[Aria_Character_Bible_v1]]

### Polly (STABLE)
- **Role:** Sentient raven intelligence, co-equal guide, archivist, meta-narrator
- **NOT:** A pet, familiar, or subordinate
- **Partnership:** Cave encounter with Izack (T1), co-equal from day one
- **True nature:** Revealed during Night of Broken Wands saving refugee children (unrevealed specifics)
- **Gen 2 title:** "Cardinal Polly" - diplomatic authority during Great Schism
- **Archive role:** Living Codex, runic memory feathers, experiential library influence

### Alexander Thorne (STABLE)
- **Role:** Prodigy bridge figure, unusual resonance to realm consciousness
- **Parents:** Izack + Aria, born ~Izack age 124
- **Arc:** Living Prophecy heir -> "95 Theses of Collaborative Casting" -> Winter King -> Ascension
- **Tragedy:** Wife Caelynn murdered by Dominion zealots during childbirth -> becomes cold ruler
- **Key relationships:** Shimmer (dragon partner), Malzeth'irun (integration bridge)
- **Cross-ref:** [[Timeline Spine]] T5

### Zara Millwright (STABLE)
- **Role:** First student, time-fracture entrant, senior leader
- **Entry:** Unstable time-magic context (T3)
- **Arc:** First student -> Widow General -> Northern Campaign leader
- **Tragedy:** Husband Marcus Brennan killed at Wedding Massacre
- **Daughter:** Lyra Netweave (betrays Alexander, executed in Gen 2)
- **Granddaughter:** Aria Millwright-Chen (Gen 3 hero)

### Count Aldric Ravencrest (LOCKED)
- **Role:** Aria's father, House Ravencrest head
- **Profile:** Studious, good-natured, slightly stern
- **Backstory:** Lost wife to magical blight, dedicated house to Nature and Lore
- **Death:** Siege of Seven Spires, redeems family honor saving Aria from his own allies
- **Note:** Treat Eldrin and Aldric as SEPARATE unless merge evidence appears

### Eldrin (STABLE but ambiguous)
- **Role:** Izack's dimensional-scholar mentor
- **Ambiguity:** Was listed as Aria's father in some sources (resolved: father = Aldric)
- **Status:** Separate character from Aldric per canon patch

### Clay (PROPOSED)
- **Role:** Loyal protector, companion
- **Tongue alignment:** RU (ancestral memory, deep roots)
- **Status:** Role pending full source confirmation

### Malzeth'irun (STABLE)
- **Role:** Demon-realm integration, bridges worlds
- **Arc:** Sheltered by Avalon (~T7), introduces demon-realm integration stakes
- **Relationship:** Integration bridge pair with Alexander
- **Romance (PROPOSED):** Malzeth'irun x Thomas Wright, cross-species love

### World Tree (STABLE)
- **Role:** Living anchor, conscious entity, realm stability and prophecy
- **Function:** Governance substrate for Avalon
- **Connection:** Alexander has direct interface/translation ability
