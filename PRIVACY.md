# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-03

This privacy policy covers AetherCode, AetherBrowse, and related SCBE-AETHERMOORE mobile or web app builds published by Issac Davis.

## Summary

The app is designed as a local-first AI workspace. It does not sell personal data and does not include advertising.

Some features may connect to third-party AI providers, GitHub, Hugging Face, local Ollama servers, or other services when you choose to use those features. Those providers may process the prompts, files, or messages you send through them under their own terms and privacy policies.

## Data You Provide

Depending on which features you use, the app may process:

- prompts and chat messages;
- code snippets or documents you paste into the app;
- provider names or model choices;
- bring-your-own-key configuration values;
- local app settings;
- generated responses, logs, or export files.

## API Keys

If the app asks for an API key, the key is intended to stay on your device or in your own configured runtime. Do not enter keys into untrusted builds.

The app should not publish, sell, or intentionally transmit raw API keys to SCBE-AETHERMOORE servers. When a third-party provider is called, your request may include the credential required by that provider.

## Third-Party AI Providers

When you choose to route a request to an external provider, the content of that request may be sent to that provider. Examples may include OpenAI-compatible providers, Anthropic-compatible providers, Google AI, Hugging Face, GitHub, Kimi, Moonshot, local Ollama-compatible endpoints, or other configured endpoints.

Review each provider's privacy policy before sending sensitive content.

## Local Storage

The app may store settings, cached web assets, conversation state, or exported files on your device. You can remove app data using your operating system's app storage controls.

## Analytics And Ads

The current app design does not require advertising identifiers and does not intentionally include third-party ad tracking.

If analytics are added in a future release, this policy should be updated before that release is published.

## Children

The app is intended for developer, productivity, research, and educational use. It is not directed to children.

## Security

The project attempts to keep secrets out of source control and generated release artifacts. You are responsible for protecting your own device, provider accounts, and API keys.

Do not use the app to handle highly sensitive data unless you understand which local and third-party services are active.

## Data Deletion

You can delete local app data through Android app settings or by uninstalling the app. For data sent to third-party providers, use that provider's account or privacy controls.

## Contact

For privacy questions or security reports, contact:

- Email: `issdandavis@gmail.com`

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The effective date above identifies the current version.
